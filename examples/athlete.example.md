# Athlete Profile — Alex Rivera

## Rider Profile

Alex is a 72kg endurance-oriented cyclist who has been riding since 2021 and racing since 2024. Background in cross-country running through college, transitioned to cycling after a knee injury made running painful. Started structured training with power in early 2024.

### Rider Type

Steady-state specialist. Strong time trialist profile — can hold threshold power for long periods with low variability. Weaker in surges and short punchy efforts, which limits performance in road races and criteriums. Good climbing ability for weight but lacks the snap to follow attacks.
